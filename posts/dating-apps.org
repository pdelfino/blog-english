Well, several reasons. The coolest one is simple. I am a romantic.
Dating apps kill the romance!

Just imagine the conversation at the *golden wedding anniversary* with
people asking how that lovely grey-haired couple met.

- How did you guys meet? School?
- No.
- Mutual friends?
- No.
- Work?
- No.
- Neighbors?
- No.
- Gym? Night club? Bar? Dance class? Painting class? Party?
- No.
- Randomly on the street? Bus? Subway?
- No. We met through a digital platform that behaves like digital
  cocaine playing dopamine triggers while humans try to @@html:<s>@@mate@@html:</s>@@ find romantic partners.
