**  Back to the future

Although I used a similar technique of Kafka on Metamorphosis (since I
started with the climax (damn, this is getting so unintentionally
sexual)), let's go back in time to humanize myself. My therapist said
that I /need/ to humanize myself more.

The exercise started with a bunch of random magazines, paper, pens,
and utensils to paint. I thought it would be cool to just use images from the magazines.

Thankfully, no Playboy magazine was available (although vintage storages in Montreal love them, don't ask me why since supposedly they sell 2nd hand chic clothes... /Sin City/...). But, all other magazines had a pattern. A pattern my single dude (/célibataire/ in French, fun word, I know) easily notices: pretty women. So, I decided to craft my collage about that.

In my defense, I started like this. On purpose, yes. After all, what I want is a /wife/. See, I am not a womanizer.

Because she symbolizes /the Queen/ I want in my life. And that I want to grow old with her. Finally, she is on top of *all the other ones*. Another cool detail about my craft is the fact that I designed it to be overflowing of girls. Just like in my mind. Not really in my life. Even less in my heart.

A detail noticed by my teacher was my emphasis on female lower body
haha. She did not pick elegant words, but I will keep things here
classy. Yeah, glutes & "coxudinha" are my Achilles heels. I am
Brazilian, after all. And I /back-end/ (/back/..../end/....) software
engineer (ba dum tss!)!
