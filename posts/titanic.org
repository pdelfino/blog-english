I went to a terrarium-building event and came back with my first
plant, a succulent. For me, it is an /allegory/ of care, resilience,
juiciness, and growth.

I like to keep it in my field of vision. It feels peaceful. It needs
very minimal care, and succulents are resilient if I forget to water
it.

I am baptizing this plant as /Titanic/... Why?

The main reason for "pet plants" to die is too much water. My friend
Lucas who is also my roommate is a serial killer of plants, btw. Since
I don't want to be like him, and I already get people saying that I
[[file:you-look-like-joe-goldberg.org][look like Joe Goldberg]], calling it Titanic is a good ironic name /for
me/.
