I like to keep it in my field of vision. It feels peaceful. It also
needs some care and maintenance, but very minimal. Succulents are very
resilient (if I end up forgetting to water it). They exhale a little
bit of O_{2}.

I usually give nicknames a lot. This has become even more extreme as I
live with Lucas, who has been my friend for a long time and also has
this habit.

Thus, I am baptizing this plant as /Titanic/... Why?

Well, educational reasons and risk management, tbh. The main reason
for "pet plants" to die is too much water. Lucas is a serial killer,
btw. Since I don't want to be like him, and I already get people
saying that I [[file:you-look-like-joe-goldberg.org][look like Joe Goldberg]], calling it Titanic is a good
ironic name /for me/.
